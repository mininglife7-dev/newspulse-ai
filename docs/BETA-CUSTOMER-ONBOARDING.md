## Part 1: Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to **https://newspulse-ai.vercel.app**
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. Enter your work email address
4. Click **"Send verification link"**
5. Check your email (including spam folder) for verification from NewsPulse
6. Click the verification link
7. You'll be redirected to create your workspace

### Step 2: Create Your Workspace

A workspace is your team's isolated news intelligence center. You'll create one during first signup.

**Workspace Setup Form:**
- **Workspace Name:** Your company or team name (e.g., "Acme Marketing")
- **Company Name:** Legal company name
- **Industry:** Select from list (e.g., Technology, Healthcare, Finance)

**What Happens Next:**
- Your workspace is created and ready to use
- You're automatically the workspace owner
- You can invite team members to collaborate (see "Inviting Team Members" below)

### Step 3: Verify Your Workspace

After creating your workspace:
1. You'll see your workspace dashboard
2. Confirm your workspace name appears in the top-left
3. You should see a "News Search" button in the main area
4. Try a test search to verify everything is working

**First-Time Verification Checklist:**
- ✓ Email verified
- ✓ Workspace created with your name
- ✓ Dashboard loads without errors
- ✓ At least one test search completes successfully

## Part 2: Using NewsPulse AI

### Running Your First News Search

1. **Enter a search topic** in the search box (e.g., "artificial intelligence regulation", "renewable energy adoption")
2. **Click "Search"**
3. **Wait 30-60 seconds** while NewsPulse:
   - Searches 50+ news sources for relevant articles
   - Summarizes each article using OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini
   - Stores results in your workspace history
4. **Review the results:**
   - Each result shows: Article title, source, publication date, AI-generated summary
   - Click on a source to read the full article
5. **Save important searches** by noting the timestamp

### Understanding Your Results

**What Each Field Means:**

| Field | Meaning | How to Use |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Title** | Original article headline | Click to verify relevance |
| **Source** | News outlet that published | Assess source credibility |
| **Date** | When published | Check how recent the news is |
| **Summary** | AI-generated 2-3 sentence overview | Quick understanding without reading full article |

**AI Summary Quality Notes:**
- Summaries are generated by GPT-4o-mini, trained on text data through April 2024
- Summaries focus on key facts and figures
- For nuanced analysis or opinions, read the full article
- Some technical jargon may be simplified or translated

### Accessing Your Search History

1. Click **"View History"** in your dashboard
2. See all past searches sorted by most recent first
3. Click any past search to view results again
4. Results are stored for the entire Beta period (July-August 2026)

**History Features:**
- View all searches from your account
- See when each search was run
- Replay results without re-running the search
- Results persist even if you sign out and back in

### Exporting Search Results

**How to Save Results:**
1. Run a search
2. **Take a screenshot** (All browsers support this)
3. **Copy results** into your note-taking app (Notion, OneNote, Google Docs)
4. Email summaries to team members directly from your email

**Note:** Beta does not yet support export to CSV or PDF (coming in General Availability).

## Part 3: Inviting Team Members

### Add Team Members to Your Workspace

As the workspace owner, you can invite other team members:

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon, top-right)
2. Click **"Team Members"**
3. Click **"Invite member"**
4. Enter their email address
5. Select their role (see roles below)
6. Click **"Send invite"**

They'll receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

### Team Roles

| Role | Can Search? | Can View History? | Can Invite Members? | Can Delete Workspace? |
|------|-------------|-------------------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| **Owner** | ✓ Yes | ✓ Yes | ✓ Yes | ✓ Yes |
| **Editor** | ✓ Yes | ✓ Yes | ✗ No | ✗ No |
| **Viewer** | ✗ No | ✓ Yes | ✗ No | ✗ No |

**Role Recommendations:**
- **Owner:** Primary contact, can manage workspace
- **Editor:** Team members who run searches
- **Viewer:** Stakeholders who review findings only

## Part 4: Best Practices

### Search Tips for Best Results

**Effective Searches (Will Yield Many Results):**
- "Machine learning in healthcare"
- "Electric vehicle market growth"
- "Cryptocurrency regulation 2026"

**Too Narrow (May Return Few Results):**
- "CEO of Company X resigned" (specific events)
- "Article about John Smith" (person names without context)

**Better Search Formulation:**
- Use 2-4 key words
- Focus on topics, not specific names
- Include time context if relevant ("2026 AI trends")

### Managing Your Searches

**Recommended Workflow:**
1. **Plan your searches:** Know what topics you need to research
2. **Run searches in batches:** Complete multiple related searches in one session
3. **Review results while fresh:** Summary context is most useful immediately after search
4. **Take notes:** Screenshot or copy important findings
5. **Archive searches:** Refer to history when needed for follow-up

### Privacy & Data Handling

**What We Collect:**
- Your search queries and topics
- When you search
- Which results you access
- Your workspace settings

**What We Do NOT Collect:**
- Mouse movements or scroll positions
- How long you read each article
- Which external links you click
- Your browsing history outside NewsPulse

**Data Retention:**
- Search history: Retained for entire Beta period
- After Beta ends: Data export available, then deletion (unless continued on paid plan)
- Your account can be deleted anytime via Settings → Account

## Part 5: Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### "I didn't receive a verification email"

**What to Try:**
1. Wait 2-3 minutes (emails can be delayed)
2. Check your spam/junk folder
3. Look for sender: `noreply@supabase.io`
4. Try signing up again with the same email

**Still not working?**
- Email us: beta-support@newspulse-ai.com
- Include the email you tried to sign up with
- We'll manually verify your account within 2 hours

#### "Search is taking too long (>2 minutes)"

**What to Try:**
1. Refresh the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
2. Wait another 30 seconds
3. Try a simpler search term
4. Close and re-open in a new tab

**If it still times out:**
- Email us with the search term you tried
- Try again in 15 minutes (our backend may be under heavy load)

#### "I'm getting an error: 'Service temporarily unavailable'"

**What to Try:**
1. Check our status page: https://status.newspulse-ai.com
2. Wait 5-10 minutes and try again
3. Try a different search term

**What We're Checking:**
- If this is widespread, our monitoring systems automatically notify our team
- We typically resolve within 15 minutes
- If it persists >30 minutes, email us immediately

#### "Results look incomplete or outdated"

**Important Notes:**
- News sources are indexed in real-time (within hours of publication)
- Some sources may have paywalls or access restrictions
- Very recent news (within 1 hour) may not be indexed yet
- Results may vary based on search specificity

**To Get Better Results:**
- Try broader search terms
- Wait a few hours for very recent news to index
- Search for related topics (not just exact phrases)

#### "I can't log back in"

**What to Try:**
1. Make sure you're using the email you signed up with
2. Click "Sign In" → enter your email → click "Send sign-in link"
3. Check your email for the sign-in link (same place as verification email)
4. The link is valid for 24 hours

**Forgot which email you used?**
- Email us: beta-support@newspulse-ai.com
- We can tell you which email is associated with your account

## Part 6: Giving Feedback

### How to Report Issues

**Report a Bug or Problem:**
1. Email: beta-support@newspulse-ai.com
2. Subject: "Beta Bug Report: [Brief description]"
3. Include:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - The exact search term (if search-related)
   - Screenshot (if helpful)
   - When it happened (date and time)

**Example:**
```
Subject: Beta Bug Report: Search results showing duplicate articles

I ran a search for "renewable energy" and the results included the same 
article from CNN twice with different summaries. It happened on July 12 
at 2:30 PM UTC.

Attachment: screenshot.png
```

### Suggest a Feature

**Request a New Feature:**
1. Email: beta-feedback@newspulse-ai.com
2. Subject: "Beta Feature Request: [Feature name]"
3. Include:
   - What you want to do
   - Why it would be useful
   - How often you'd use it

**Example:**
```
Subject: Beta Feature Request: Schedule recurring searches

I'd like to set up automatic daily searches for "AI news" so I get 
summarized articles every morning in my email. This would save me time 
instead of manually searching each day.
```

### Share Your Beta Experience

**Help Us Improve:**
- Monthly surveys (we'll email you)
- Optional 15-minute call with product team
- Early access to new features for feedback
- Special recognition in Beta release notes
