# 🧬 CEIS — Founder Guide

You are the Chief Evolution Officer. CEIS does the reading; you make the calls.
Nothing enters the Cathedral without your approval — the system is built so it
_cannot_ approve itself.

## Your weekly ritual (~15 minutes)

1. **Open `/evolution`** every Monday (the cycle runs automatically at 06:00 UTC Monday).
2. **Read the top of the report**: Overall Evolution Score, discoveries, customer insights, and — just as important — the _Intentionally Ignored_ list. If the immune system rejected something you disagree with, that is a signal to recalibrate, not to override silently.
3. **Triage the DNA queue.** Each card shows its Evolution Score (0–100), priority, effort, mission, and evidence. For anything interesting, click **Start Review**.
4. **Work the nine quality gates.** Pass or fail each gate on the card (architecture, business, security, compliance, testing, founder alignment, customer value, evidence, performance). Add notes — they are stored.
5. **Approve or Reject.**
   - **Approve** only unlocks when all nine gates pass. Approval writes a `successful-idea` into the genome.
   - **Reject** asks you _why_. Your reason is stored permanently — CEIS will not re-propose the idea unless meaningfully stronger evidence appears (confidence +0.15 over what it had).

## How to read the numbers

| Metric                  | Meaning                                  | Healthy                                                  |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Evolution Score**     | Mean quality of this cycle's proposals   | 55–80. 0 with an empty queue = quiet week, which is fine |
| **Architecture Health** | 100 minus failed gates and queue backlog | ≥ 85                                                     |
| **Evidence Confidence** | How well-sourced the active queue is     | ≥ 60                                                     |
| **Learning Velocity**   | Genome entries added in 7 days           | > 0 every week                                           |
| **Knowledge Genome**    | Total permanent memory entries           | grows monotonically                                      |

## What CEIS will never do

- Copy a competitor's product, code, design, or brand — it extracts _principles_ only.
- Generate DNA for something the Cathedral already has, or that duplicates work in the queue.
- Implement anything. CEIS proposes; humans dispose.
- Forget. Every rejection, approval, and lesson is permanent genome memory.

## A quiet week is a feature

If a cycle produces zero proposals, the immune system and evidence thresholds
did their job. Evolution driven by weak evidence is drift, not progress.
