# Customer Feedback Collection Templates

**Purpose:** Ready-to-deploy feedback forms for customer pilot phase  
**For:** Founder to send to customers during and after pilot  
**Audience:** First 5 customers in pilot cohort  
**Timeline:** Weekly feedback (weeks 1-4) + end-of-pilot survey (week 4)

---

## Overview

Systematic feedback collection serves three purposes:
1. **Unblock customers** — Identify and fix issues quickly
2. **Validate product** — Confirm if the 3-step onboarding works
3. **Inform next phase** — Data for German localization decision and product roadmap

This document contains 4 ready-to-use feedback templates:
1. **Weekly check-in** (sent every Monday during pilot)
2. **Onboarding blockers** (sent if customer gets stuck)
3. **Feature request collection** (weekly)
4. **End-of-pilot summary** (week 4)

## How to Deploy These Templates

### Setup (30 min, one-time)

1. **Create 4 Google Forms** using templates 1A, 2A, 3A, 4A above
2. **Get shareable links** for each form
3. **Add links to email templates** (replace [Insert Google Form link])
4. **Save as Gmail drafts** or store in a "EURO AI Templates" folder
5. **Set calendar reminders** for weekly sends:
   - Monday 9 AM: Weekly check-in
   - Friday 3 PM: Feature request
   - End of week 4: End-of-pilot survey

### Sending (5 min per send)

1. **Open template** (email or form link)
2. **Customize** [Customer Name] and any step-specific text
3. **Send** to customer
4. **Note in spreadsheet** when sent (so you can track response rates)

### Collecting & Analyzing (15 min per week)

**Monday after sending:**
1. Open Google Form responses
2. Scan for blockers (responses that indicate "stuck")
3. Flag those customers for same-day follow-up call
4. Note patterns:
   - Are multiple customers stuck on same step?
   - Is a specific field confusing everyone?
   - Are there recurring feature requests?

**Friday after feature request:**
1. Review feature requests
2. Group by theme (authentication, integrations, reporting, etc.)
3. Note which features requested by multiple customers (signals priority)

**Week 4 after end-of-pilot survey:**
1. Export all responses
2. Calculate NPS (Net Promoter Score):
   - Promoters (9-10): Count
   - Passives (7-8): Count
   - Detractors (1-6): Count
   - NPS = (Promoters - Detractors) / Total × 100
3. Summarize themes from open-ended questions
4. Identify top 3 pain points and top 3 strengths

## What to Do With Feedback

### Immediate Actions (Same day)

- **Customer is blocked:** Reach out within 2 hours with help
- **Email verification failing:** Check Supabase email config
- **Data entry confusing:** Offer to do it together via call

### Weekly Actions (Friday review)

- **Recurring complaint:** Add to product backlog as bug fix
- **Common feature request:** Add to roadmap as consideration
- **Performance issue:** Escalate to engineering if systemic

### Monthly Actions (End of pilot)

- **Compile NPS score:** Determine if product resonates
- **Map to success criteria:** Did we hit completion targets? (see CUSTOMER-SUCCESS-PLAYBOOK.md)
- **Inform next phase:** Use feedback to decide German localization priority and roadmap

## Sample Response to Common Feedback

### "Email verification not working"

**Response (within 2 hours):**

```
Hi [Customer],

Thanks for letting me know. Email verification can be tricky. Here's what to try:

1. Check your spam folder — verification emails sometimes land there
2. If no email there, click "Resend verification email" on the signup page
3. Check spam folder again
4. If still nothing, let me know and I'll manually verify for you

Let me know how it goes — happy to help.

—
[Your Name]
```

### "Adding AI systems is confusing"

**Response (same day, ideally via call):**

```
Hi [Customer],

I'm sorry the AI systems step is unclear. Would it help to jump on a quick 15-min call? I can walk you through it and show you how to structure your systems.

Or, here's a quick guide:
[Add specific examples for their industry]

Either way, let's make sure you can finish this step today.

—
[Your Name]
```

### "Would love to see team collaboration"

**Response (within 24 hours):**

```
Hi [Customer],

Great suggestion — team collaboration is on our roadmap for Q3 2026. A lot of customers have asked for this.

For now, if you want to involve teammates, you can share your login credentials in a password manager. Not ideal, but it works.

We'll definitely build proper team features. When we do, you'll be one of the first to know.

Thanks for the feedback!

—
[Your Name]
```

## Success Metrics

Feedback collection is successful when:

- ✅ ≥80% of customers respond to weekly check-ins
- ✅ You identify and help resolve blockers within 2 hours
- ✅ You can list top 3 pain points by week 2
- ✅ You can list top 3 feature requests by week 3
- ✅ End-of-pilot NPS ≥ 30 (good for early stage)
- ✅ ≥70% would recommend to colleague
- ✅ ≥60% want to continue using after pilot
