**Timeline:** Get started this week, complete in 1-2 weeks.

Three things:
1. **Honest feedback** — Tell us what works and what doesn't
2. **Your time** — A 30-minute kickoff call this week, then weekly check-ins (optional but recommended)
3. **Real data** — Use it with your actual AI systems (or test data if you prefer)

1. **Sign up:** [Visit EURO AI](https://euro-ai.vercel.app)
2. **Create account** with your email
3. **Verify email** (link in inbox)

It's self-guided, but I'm here to help.

### Questions Before You Start?

Feel free to reply to this email or schedule a quick call with me.

**Let's do this!**

## Customization Tips

### Personalization
- Use customer's name
- Reference past conversations
- Mention specific pain points they've shared

### Urgency (Light Touch)
- "First 5 spots"
- "Limited beta"
- "Starting this week"

### Clear CTA
- Direct link to signup
- Calendar link for meeting
- Reply-to email address

### Social Proof (When Available)
- "Already used by [Customer 1], [Customer 2]"
- "Mentioned in [Publication]"
- "Built by team from [Previous Companies]"

### Tone
- Friendly, not corporate
- Clear value proposition
- Acknowledge their time investment
- Show you care about feedback

## FAQ to Answer Before Sending

**Q: What if they ask about pricing?**  
A: "We're in beta, so it's free for you. After beta, we're planning simple, transparent pricing. I'll keep you posted."

**Q: What if they're worried about data privacy?**  
A: "Your data is encrypted and isolated in your workspace. We follow GDPR standards. [Link to privacy policy]"

**Q: What if they ask about the product roadmap?**  
A: "We're starting with AI inventory and risk assessment. Next features based on customer feedback will likely be team collaboration and evidence tracking."

**Q: Can they use test data instead of real AI systems?**  
A: "Absolutely. Start with test data if you prefer, switch to real systems later."
