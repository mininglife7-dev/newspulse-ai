# EURO AI Quick Start Guide
## Get Productive in 15 Minutes

Welcome to EURO AI! This guide gets you started with your first AI governance workflow.

---

## 5 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Log In (1 minute)

1. Go to: `https://euro-ai.production.url`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll land on your **Dashboard**

**What you see:**
- Your workspace name (top left)
- Quick stats (AI Systems, Assessments, Evidence)
- Recent activity feed
- Navigation menu

---

### Step 2: Meet Your Workspace (2 minutes)

Your workspace is your **isolated environment** where:
- Only your team can see data
- All your AI systems and assessments live
- Audit trail records every action
- Role-based access controls RLS policies enforce data isolation

**Menu Overview:**
```
Dashboard          ← You are here (overview)
AI Systems         ← Add and catalog AI systems
Risk Assessments   ← Evaluate compliance risks
Evidence           ← Upload compliance documents
Remediation        ← Track fixing issues
Settings           ← Manage team and workspace
```

---

### Step 3: Add Your First AI System (5 minutes)

1. Click **"AI Systems"** in the menu
2. Click **"+ Add System"** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Name:** (e.g., "ChatGPT Integration for Customer Support")
   - **Vendor:** (e.g., "OpenAI")
   - **Use Case:** Select from dropdown (e.g., "Customer Service")
   - **EU AI Act Risk:** Select level (Prohibited / High / Limited / Minimal)
   - **Key Features:** (e.g., "Handles customer inquiries 24/7, escalates to humans")
4. Click **"Save"**
5. You'll see your system in the list below

**✅ Success:** Your AI system is now in the inventory!

---

### Step 4: Create Your First Risk Assessment (5 minutes)

1. Click **"Risk Assessments"**
2. Click **"+ New Assessment"**
3. Select the AI system you just created
4. Answer the compliance questions (10-15 questions):
   - Have you documented the system's purpose?
   - Do you have data protection measures in place?
   - Is there human oversight of decisions?
   - etc.
5. For each question, select: Yes / No / Partial / N/A
6. **"Save as Draft"** when you're done
7. Come back to this assessment to add evidence and mark "In Review"

**✅ Success:** Your first risk assessment is created!

---

### Step 5: Upload Compliance Evidence (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Evidence"**
2. Click **"+ Upload Document"**
3. Select the assessment you just created (from Step 4)
4. Upload a document:
   - PDF, image, or text file
   - Max 10MB
   - Examples: "Governance Policy.pdf", "Data Flow Diagram.png"
5. Add description (e.g., "Internal policy document covering AI oversight")
6. Click **"Upload"**

**✅ Success:** Your first evidence document is uploaded!

---

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit a system after creating it?**  
A: Yes! Click the system, then "Edit". Changes are logged in the audit trail.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**  
A: All changes are tracked in the audit trail. The platform doesn't delete history — it records everything for compliance.

**Q: Can my team see my data?**  
A: Only people in your workspace can see your data. Each workspace is completely isolated (multi-tenant RLS).

**Q: What if I need help?**  
A: Click the **"Help"** button (?) in the top right, or email us at [support email].

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Workspace** | Your isolated environment where your team works together |
| **AI System** | An AI tool or application your organization uses |
| **Risk Assessment** | Evaluation of compliance risks for each AI system |
| **Evidence** | Documentation supporting your compliance (policies, audits, etc.) |
| **Audit Trail** | Record of every action taken in the workspace (who, what, when) |
| **RLS** | Row-Level Security — database feature ensuring data isolation |

## Performance Tips

- **Bulk upload:** Can't upload multiple documents at once? Use the batch upload feature in Settings
- **Export data:** Backing up? Use Settings → Export to download your data as CSV
- **Filter results:** Use the search/filter bar to find specific systems or assessments
- **Sort columns:** Click column headers to sort by date, name, risk level, etc.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't log in | Check email inbox for password reset link. If missing, check spam folder. |
| Form won't submit | Ensure all required fields are filled (marked with *). Check browser console. |
| Data not appearing | Refresh the page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R). Clear browser cache if persistent. |
| Slow performance | Are you in a large workspace (1000+ records)? Try using date filters to load specific period. |
