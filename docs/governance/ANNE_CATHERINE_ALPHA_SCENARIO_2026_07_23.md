## THE CUSTOMER CHALLENGE

**Anne Catherine** runs a mid-sized accounting firm in Frankfurt with 25 staff. The firm specializes in AI-driven financial analysis for German SMEs.

**Her problem:**

Six months ago, Anne Catherine deployed three AI systems:
1. **Document Classifier** — Automatically categorizes invoices and receipts (medium risk)
2. **Tax Optimizer** — Recommends tax strategies based on client data (high risk)
3. **Client Matching Engine** — Suggests which clients might need specific services (medium risk)

The EU AI Act came into force. Anne Catherine faces this question:

*"Are my AI systems compliant? If a regulator audits us, can I prove it?"*

She's tried spreadsheets. She's called a consultant. Both approaches left her with:
- Disconnected documentation
- No clear audit trail
- Uncertainty about what "compliance" actually means
- Fear of fines and reputational damage
- No ability to track progress
